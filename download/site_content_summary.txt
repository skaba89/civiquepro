5. OFFICIAL EXAM FORMAT & THEMATIC BREAKDOWN
================================================================================

EXAM PARAMETERS (per arrêté du 10 octobre 2025):
- Total questions: 40
- Passing score: 32/40 (80%)
- Time limit: 45 minutes
- Format: Digital (on computer at approved center)
- Scoring: 1 point per correct answer, 0 for wrong/missing
- Applies from: January 1, 2026
- Target candidates: CSP (carte de séjour pluriannuelle), CR (carte de résident), 
  naturalization

QUESTION TYPES:
1. Questions de connaissance (28 questions): Test verifiable facts - dates, institutions, 
   principles, symbols. "Either you know it or you don't."
2. Mises en situation (12 questions): Describe a daily life context; candidate must 
   identify applicable right/republican value. Only in "Principes et valeurs" (6) and
